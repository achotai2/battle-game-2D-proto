Problem: Tactics should handle attack messages.
- I don't neccessarily want animation telling movement to unfreeze when animation_finished triggers. This could cause conflicts later.
- I want tactical to handle attacks. Weapon tells tactical there is a target and sends attack info, tactical decides what to do.
- Player tactical can also handle freezing attacks when moving, and passing on controls messages to movement maybe.
- Tactical could then handle unfreeze actions upon animation finished maybe.
Solution
- Move attack signal to tactical, and have it make the decision and pass on message to animation, target, movement, etc.
- animation_finished signal from animation goes back to tactical for analysis to unfreeze movement.
- Player controls sends signal to player tactical which handles freezing and unfreezing movement.
